# Delete Your Account

**Last Updated:** January 22, 2026

## How to Delete Your Account

To permanently delete your Today Matters account and all associated data:

1. Open the **Today Matters** app on your device
2. Navigate to **Profile** (bottom navigation)
3. Tap **Settings** (or the settings icon)
4. Scroll to **Account** section
5. Tap **Delete Account**
6. Confirm the deletion when prompted

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

- **Account Information**: Email address, name, authentication credentials
- **User Preferences**: Goals, values, routines, and app settings
- **Location Data**: All location samples and history
- **Screen Time Data**: App usage statistics and screen time information
- **Calendar Events**: All synced calendar events and planned activities
- **Communication Data**: Email metadata and communication history
- **Health Data**: Steps, heart rate, sleep, and workout information
- **Activity Patterns**: All stored activity patterns and insights

## Data Retention Period

After you request account deletion, your data will be permanently deleted within **30 days**. During this period, your account will be deactivated and you will not be able to access it.

## Data That Cannot Be Deleted

Some anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes, but this data cannot be linked back to your personal identity.
